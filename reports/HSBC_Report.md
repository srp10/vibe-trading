## Part 1: Fundamental Business & Financial Health Analysis

### Business Model Deconstruction

**Core Business:**
HSBC Holdings plc is one of the world's largest banking and financial services organizations. The company operates through various segments, including Retail Banking and Wealth Management, Commercial Banking, Global Banking and Markets, and Global Private Banking. HSBC generates revenue primarily through interest income from loans, fees from banking services, and investment income.

**Economic Moat:**
HSBC's primary economic moat lies in its extensive global network, strong brand recognition, and diversified service offerings. The bank's international presence allows it to serve multinational corporations and high-net-worth individuals effectively, creating a competitive advantage through scale and customer relationships.
